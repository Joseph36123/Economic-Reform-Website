1997/09/12: The 15th National Congress of the Communist Party of China

The Fifteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China identified Deng Xiaoping Theory as the guiding ideology of the CCP. The congress also established the rule of law as the basic strategy for governing the country, adhered to public ownership as the main body. It was insisted that distribution according to work should be the norm. The conference determined China's basic economic system and distribution system in the primary stage of socialism. The report to the 15th National Congress of the Communist Party of China also identified intellectuals as part of the working class, as a remedy of the discrimination and persecutive that the Chinese government imposed on intellectual in the Anti-Rightist movement and the Cultural Revolution.
