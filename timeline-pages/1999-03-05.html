1999/03/05: The Second Session of the Ninth National People's Congress

The Second Session of the Ninth National People's Congress enshrined Deng Xiaoping Theory into the Chinese Constitution. The meeting was held from March 5 to 16, 1999. First, Premier Zhu Rongji made a government work report, and then Zeng Peiyan, director of the State Planning Commission, made a report on the implementation of the national economic and social development plan in 1998 and a draft plan report in 1999. Minister Xiang Huaicheng made a report on the implementation of the central and local budgets in 1998, and Vice Chairman Tian Jiyun made an explanation on the draft amendment to the Constitution of the People's Republic of China.
