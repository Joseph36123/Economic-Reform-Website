2018/03/05: The First Session of the Thirteenth National People's Congress

The Thirteenth National People's Congress passed an amendment to the Constitution to remove term limits for the President and Vice-President. Some people believe that this move ended the "abolition of the lifelong leadership positions" that was implemented in the Deng Xiaoping era. It not only destroyed the collective leadership system and democratic centralism, but also gave Xi Jinping the opportunity to become a lifelong leader and dictator after Mao Zedong.
