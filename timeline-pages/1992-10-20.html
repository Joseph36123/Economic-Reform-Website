1992/10/20: The Fourteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China

The main task of this congress is to use Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics as a guide, summarize the practical experience of the 14 years since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee, and determine that the goal of China's economic system reform is to establish a socialist market economy system. On behalf of the 13th Central Committee, Jiang Zemin delivered a report titled "Accelerating the pace of reform, opening up and modernization to achieve greater success in the cause of socialism with Chinese characteristics." This congress requires the whole party to seize the opportunity and speed up economic development. Most importantly, the conference incorporated the theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics into the Chinese constitution.
